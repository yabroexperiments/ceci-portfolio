## Role

UI design for the on-device firmware. Worked with the industrial design team to ensure the screen content respected the constrained physical proportions of the device.

## Constraints

- Two-line monochrome screen, ~2 inch diagonal
- T9-style numeric keypad as primary input
- Battery target: one week standby, so the always-on display had to be radically minimal

## What I Learned

Designing for a hardware constraint is the opposite of designing for the open web — every pixel and every bit of energy budget matters. Lessons here directly informed how I approach mobile-first design at Binance today.
