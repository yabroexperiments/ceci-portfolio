<Section title="My Role" kicker="Role">

**Design Lead and Product Designer**

I led the project and designed the Copy Trading App from scratch with bi-weekly iterations and pushed to launch within 2 months.

I am responsible for a smooth user experience and achieve growth KPI targets each quarter.

</Section>

<Section title="The Challenge" kicker="Challenge">

The Copy Trading feature needs to satisfy the needs of various user personas as there are many different feedback from users and I led the discussion between Designers, PM and MKT teams to reach the suitable solution catering for:

- Advanced user requirements of professional Lead Traders and seasoned Copy Traders
- Easily follow or copy the order with one click for beginner Copy Traders
- Enable Lead Traders and Copy Traders to control their portfolio status at any time

</Section>

<Section title="Competitive Product Research" kicker="Research">

I conducted extensive research of the competition with constant monitoring of new features. I proposed simpler and more intuitive processes and emphasized our strengths.

</Section>
