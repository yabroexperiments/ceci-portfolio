## Role

UI / Visual design — concept exploration for HTC Sense home-screen widgets, with an emphasis on typographic expression rather than the conventional grid of buttons.

## Approach

The brief was to find a fresh visual language for utility apps that usually feel utilitarian. I leaned into oversized numerals, ligature-like overlaps, and contrasting AM/PM weight to give each glance personality.

## Tools

Photoshop, Illustrator, AfterEffects (for the motion mockups not shown here).
