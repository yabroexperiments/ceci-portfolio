## Background

Public trader leaderboards turn invisible performance into a credible signal. The challenge: present dense numerical data clearly while preserving the high-energy aesthetic of the rest of the Binance Futures platform.

## Approach

- **Dark mode by default** — matches the Futures trading interface so leaderboards feel like a continuation, not a separate product.
- **Performance hierarchy** — ROI, PnL, win rate, drawdown all visible at a glance with size weighting that mirrors how traders evaluate.
- **Time-window control** — 7/30/90/all-time toggles let users filter by horizon.

## Outcome

The leaderboard became a top-of-funnel for the Copy Trading product — most copy-trade follow actions originate here.
