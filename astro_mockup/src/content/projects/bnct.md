## Background

Binance is the world's largest cryptocurrency exchange. The Copy Trading product lets retail users automatically replicate the strategies of high-performing traders without needing to actively manage their own trades.

## Role

I led the end-to-end UX/UI design — from research and information architecture through to high-fidelity visuals and design QA. I worked closely with PM, engineering, and the trading product team across multiple time zones.

## Key Decisions

- **Trust signals first** — performance metrics, win rate, drawdown, and follower counts are surfaced prominently so users can evaluate a trader before committing funds.
- **Risk controls** — copy amount, stop-loss, leverage caps, and trade restrictions are visible at every step of the funnel.
- **Mobile-first** — the majority of crypto trading happens on mobile, so the design language was set on phone first and adapted up to desktop.

## Outcome

The Copy Trading product launched in 2023 and became one of the most-used social trading features on Binance.
