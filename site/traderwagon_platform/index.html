TRADERWAGON

TraderWagon Social Trading - Binance 3rd Party Tool

TraderWagon is a social crypto trading platform which helps beginners find experienced traders and copy their trades. Users can quickly log in using their Binance credentials on TraderWagon, and use third-party app on Binance.

My Role

Design Lead and Product Designer I am the Design Lead for TraderWagon and led 2-4 designers to develop the product including the social media and platform experience. I managed the designers to create design guidelines, components, page flows and reviewed user experiences on the TraderWagon platform.

User Journey Map

Conducted workshops and interviews to understand the user journey and discover the processes or steps where users encounter issues. The issues found from the mapping exercise were resolved and achieved a 30% increase of users.

Binance 3rd Party Tool

This Binance 3rd Party Tool is an embedded app on the Binance homepage. Users can use the app to copy trade with his/her Binance wallet and was the most popular copy trading tool prior to Binance having its own copy trading feature.
