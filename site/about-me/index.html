Hello, I'm Ceci Chang





I am a UI/UX designer from Taiwan with more than 10 years of experiences in FinTech, Cryptocurrency and Mobile OS industries.


My most recent position is Senior Product Designer at Binance focused on the design of the cryptocurrency trading platform, and worked at Coinful Capital as UI/UX Lead prior. I graduated with a Master’s Degree in Fashion and Communication Design from Shih Chien University in Taiwan.


I am skilled to translate abstract ideas into concrete actions. I am experienced in a fast paced iterative/agile work environment and strive to deliver highest quality design assets and user experiences. I have a huge passion for solving problems and simplifying designs to create intuitive user experiences that address customer needs. In my 10+ years of design journey, I had the opportunity to work in startup and enterprise environments on UI/UX, branding, graphic design projects related to FinTech, Cryptocurrency, Mobile OS (in Mozilla and HTC) and Fashion industries.


In my spare time, I enjoy nature and sports such as diving, hiking and snowboarding. The outdoors always gives me inspiration and recharges my energy.
